Headless Commerce: A Best-of-Breed Strategy

A headless commerce approach enables brands to build a nimble solution comprised of best-of-breed components. These components work seamlessly together to create a modern ecommerce solution built on cutting-edge technology and supported by secure, powerful back-end processes. With connectors to leading CMS and DXP providers, including WordPress and Adobe, we offer you the flexibility to build a commerce ecosystem that supports your unique business.

  • Nimble development – create compelling new user experiences without having to re-develop the back-end.
  • Flexibility – quickly make updates to the content layer of the platform. Add and remove products and campaigns with ease.
  • Customization – infinite customization with complete control over the look and feel of the customer experience.
  • Control – manage the areas that you want to own, the buyer experience for example, while outsourcing areas such as payments and risk to experienced partners – like us!

Own the shopping experience.

With a flexible API architecture and growing partner network we make it easy for you to plug and play our solutions into your existing ecosystem. Our API layer facilitates the free flow of data and connections so that you can bring together the components that work best for you.

  • Shopping cart
  • Product Information Management (PIM)
  • Merchandising
  • Pricing and promotions
  • Transactional emails
