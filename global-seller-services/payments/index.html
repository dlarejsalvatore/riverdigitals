Increase Renewals and Revenue with Optimized Billing

Online payment processing that keeps your revenue flowing with our deep global acquiring network and Machine Learning to identify where and when to route recurring transactions. The industry refers to this process as intelligent transaction routing. At River Digitals, we’re experts at it.

Dynamic Retry Logic

Maximize authorization rates by using pre- and post-expiration billing attempts to resolve temporary transaction blocking issues.

Optimized Retry Timing

Uses Machine Learning to identify the best time to re-submit a transaction on a country-by-country basis. Authorization rates can vary by the hour of the day, the day of the week or month due to the timing of payroll and remittance cycles.

Account Updater

Automatically updates saved Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express card information to minimize decline rates when a card on file has expired.

Expired Card Optimization

Uses dynamic business rules and Machine Learning to update card expiration dates when account updates are not available.

Notifications and Dunning

Proactively informs customers about events (such as card expiration) that may affect their recurring card payment.
