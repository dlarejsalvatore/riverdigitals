Global Payment Types

Beyond cards, River Digitals enables a variety of alternative payment types to improve your conversions.

Cards

Cards are the most popular payment type in the world . Shoppers are able to use their debit or credit card to pay for a purchase online by inputting their card details at checkout.

Digital Wallets

Also referred to as an e-wallet, these payments allow shoppers to store a preferred payment method or bank account within a digital or mobile application.

Buy Now Pay Later

Buy Now Pay Later payments allow shoppers to purchase goods but pay for them at a later date, with instalments, financing or invoicing options based on a credit agreement.

Cash

Cash-based payment methods allow orders to be placed online yet paid in-person at a designated location, such as a convenience store, or upon delivery of the order.

Bank Transfer

With bank transfer payments, shoppers can securely pay for their purchases directly from their bank account. Bank transfers are typically processed within 3 to 4 business days.

Wire Transfer

Wire transfers provide a real-time or near real-time way to transfer payments electronically from one bank account to another.
